My high school

The days in high school is very important to me. Not only because I met a lot of great people but also because I have learned much. My high school, high school affiliated to JiaoTong University, is nothing like other schools. Teachers are not much care about students' study and there are so many interesting extracurricular activities. I have participate many of these activities, for instance, basketball match, football match as well as chorus. Since we have so many activities and not much study stress, it requires self-discipline to study well.

Also, I was very lucky to know all my teachers. All of them are very kind people. I still remember my Chinese teacher's word: I believe that as long as you make effort, you are sure to have a good result. I like my teachers because they really treated us fairly as adults. They are willing to discuss with my problems and give me their suggestions. I will always remember my high school days and these lovely people!
