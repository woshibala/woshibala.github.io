Life in NEU

I have studied and lived in NEU for over a year. NEU has a very beautiful campus. Although the campus is not that large in size, it is like a community. People live in it, study in it and work in it. You don't have to go out of the campus for something. In one word, NEU is a wonderful place for study and research.

My dormitory

Me and other three people share a room. My room mates are all very kind and always share their fruit with me. Talking after black out is also a traditional activity. Since we don't have a bathroom in the dormitory, we have to go to public bathhouse for shower. And that's a little bit inconvinient especially in winter.

NEU The Other Guys

SE

I never thought I would major in software engineering before I came to NEU. I had learned a little about programming in high school. The course I took in NEU is really attractive to me. While I was going deeper into these courses, I found myself really interesting in solving problems with computers as well as programmes.

Friendship

I am so lucky to have so many friends in NEU. My room mates, my classmates, my work mates, even someone I don't really know his/her name gave my much help. Without them I won't have the colorful and fulfilling life on this campus. I also learned much from them. These friendships are so precious that I will remember for my life time.

Teachers

No one can describe all the teachers here in one word. But the all of them are kind people and willing to help our students. My linear algebra teacher, for instance, is a teacher with patience. He always came to classroom half hour earlier to answer our questions. No matter how boring the problems are, he would explain it to us till we fully understood.
